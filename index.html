Our Refinishing Process

Our team will apply tape and mask the room before any work begins. We then strip and sand the surface, repair any imperfections or damage, apply a bonding agent and spray a professional-grade topcoat. We use low-VOC, pet and family friendly products. The room is unmasked before we leave and most jobs are ready to use within 24 hours.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about tub reglazing, tile refinishing and our services across Long Island, NY.

What areas of Long Island do you serve?

We serve all of Nassau County and Suffolk County on Long Island. This includes Roslyn Heights, Port Washington, Carle Place, Islip, Oceanside, Patchogue, East Setauket and hundreds of other communities across Long Island. If you are located in Nassau or Suffolk County, we can come out to your home or business for a free estimate.

How long does bathtub refinishing take?

Most bathtub reglazing jobs are completed the same day in just a few hours. The technician will mask the room, prep the surface, repair any damage, apply the coating and remove all tape before leaving. You can start using your tub again within 24 hours of completion. No permits, no demolition and no waiting weeks for contractors.

How much can I save by reglazing instead of replacing?

Replacing a bathtub involves the cost of permits, labor, disposal of the old tub and installation of the new one. Depending on the type of tub and the level of repair needed, Long Island homeowners can expect to save an average of 50 to 80 percent when they choose refinishing over replacement. It is one of the smartest home improvement investments you can make.

Do you offer a warranty on your refinishing work?

Yes, all of our reglazing services come backed by our industry-leading warranty. We stand behind all work that we complete. Please reach out to us to discuss the specific warranty details for your project. Our goal is for every customer to be completely happy with the finished product and to enjoy their restored bathroom for years to come.

What surfaces can you refinish?

We refinish bathtubs, tiles, shower stalls, sinks, hot tubs, jacuzzis, countertops and cabinets. We also offer specialized services including rust and chip repair, non-slip application and tub and tile color changes. We work on porcelain, ceramic, fiberglass, acrylic and other common bathroom surfaces throughout Long Island.

Service 01

Bathtub Reglazing

If you have a bathtub that is dated or damaged, our professional reglazing service transforms it right before your eyes — the same day. We handle porcelain, fiberglass and acrylic tubs throughout Nassau and Suffolk County. Our technician strips and sands the surface, repairs any chips or knicks, applies a bonding agent and sprays a high-quality professional topcoat that looks and feels brand new. The job is completed the same day and your tub is ready to use within 24 hours. All work is backed by our industry-leading warranty.

Service 02

Tile Reglazing

Tile refinishing is an easy, no-hassle, mess-free alternative to tile replacement. Restoring your cracked and chipped tiles is simple when you choose to have them refinished. The entire job is done in just one day and you will be back to using your bathroom within 24 hours from completion. You can extend the life of your bathroom wall, floor and shower tiles without spending a lot of money. Our experts reglaze wall tiles and floor tiles without removing them — in many cases there is no need for regrouting, saving you time and money.

Service 03

Shower Stall Refinishing

Your shower stall sees heavy use every day and over time the surfaces show it. Our shower stall refinishing service restores fiberglass, acrylic and tile enclosures to a bright, clean and slip-resistant finish. We remove discoloration, soap scum buildup, cracks and worn coating with a complete professional refinishing treatment. This service is especially popular with Long Island landlords and property managers who want to quickly and affordably refresh a bathroom between tenants. All shower stall refinishing work is completed the same day and backed by our warranty.

Service 04

Sink Reglazing

Sinks last a really long time and are very durable, but over time the surface can start to wear down and look dirty or dull. Rather than choosing to rip out the sink and have it replaced, you can have your sink resurfaced so that it can be restored to its beautiful luster. Sink reglazing is an easy procedure and Long Island homeowners will find that they save money when they choose restoration over replacement. Our services are designed to fit into your budget easily and we can restore your bathroom sink, kitchen basin, pedestal sink and vanity sink across Nassau and Suffolk County.

Service 05

Hot Tub & Jacuzzi Refinishing

Your hot tub or jacuzzi deserves to look its best. Our refinishing service restores faded, chipped or stained hot tub and jacuzzi surfaces to their former glory for just a fraction of the cost of a new unit. Whether the surface has dulled over years of use or needs color change, our certified team applies a high-quality professional coating that brings back the luxurious, glossy look you love. We serve residential and commercial properties throughout Long Island. Call us for a free estimate and we will schedule your hot tub or jacuzzi refinishing the same week.

Rust & Chip Repair + Color Change

Rusty tubs are our specialty — we can make yours look new again. We also offer chip and crack repair on bathtubs, tiles and sink surfaces throughout Long Island. In addition, if you are tired of the color of your tub or tiles, we offer a full tub and tile color change service.
